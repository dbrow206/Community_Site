University of North Carolina Campus Clean-up

Hosted By UNCC

Friday, October 30

10 a.m - 2 p.m

The annual campus cleanup, which is led by the Office of Waste Management and Recycling, will take place from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.,
Friday, Oct 30 (Note, this is a change from previous reports). In an effort to adhere to physical distancing guidelines, participants must
sign up online to participate. Masks are required, and gloves and hand sanitizer will be available. Bags and pickup sticks will be provided as participants arrive.
